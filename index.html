About Us

Our mission is to help people consolidate their debt and retake control of their financial lives. We work exclusively for our customers, and receive no incentives from banks or other financial institutions. Our goal is to make sure you can pay back your debt as quickly and easily as possible.

Project Description

At 2Towers, one of our largest challenges is keeping track of client data. Some client information is available through Zoho and some is available using Sharefile. We are looking for a way to centralize our work process by combining the utilities of multiple software into one. Three of our primary objectives are to be able to:

  • Track a client's progress through our proprietary process.
  • Access client information through one interface.
  • Reduce operating cost and simplify daily business processes.
